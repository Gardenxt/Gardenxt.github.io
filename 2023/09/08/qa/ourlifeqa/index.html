【ourlife】Q＆A 2023August

发表于 2023-09-08 更新于 2025-07-27 分类于 游戏

Hey everybody! Welcome to the August 2023 Patreon Q&A. It’s the end of summer, we’re moving into fall, Our life 1 is basically over at this point because I’m recording this when the beta has gone out, and Our Life 2 is ready to be in full development. It’s a very exciting time, and there were a lot of very good questions sent in these past few weeks. So let’s get right into that.

祝你愉快!你觉得Our Life 1里的男孩们会穿粉红色的衣服去看芭比电影吗?

不是他们三个一起，只有他们自己。如果MC想这么做，他们就会和MC一起去，或者像Terry和Miranda这样的一大群朋友。但他们不会单独行动，他们三个也不会像三重奏那样一起行动。

Baxter有没有和他儿时的朋友分享过那个他14岁时和他跳舞的神秘人?

他可能会这么做，但只是随口一说。他不会详细说明这件事，也不会让这件事显得特别重要，尽管他当时可能被MC迷住了，但他不会对那个年龄的朋友说这些。

嘿，首先我要再说一遍我有多喜欢这个游戏，我们能提问这些问题是多么棒。至于我的问题，在Tik Tok关于Our Life 2中，它说Qiu在第二步是悲伤的。我以为Qiu只是在那个阶段生气，而不是伤心，所以我很惊讶。他们在伤心什么?

当一个人普遍不开心时，大多数时候，它通常不是全天候的纯粹愤怒和烦恼。通常还有其他事情在困扰他们，让“他”以其他方式不开心，而不仅仅是烦恼。Qiu有很多，你知道，“他”正在处理的问题和斗争，这使得生活不像“他”年轻时那么容易。

Baxter对Franky和/或者Tamarack有过嫉妒吗?如果我没记错的话，他告诉Jamie，他觉得他和Qiu、Renee之间的年龄差距随着年龄的增长变得越来越明显。那他有没有觉得自己被取代了?

Baxter从来没有嫉妒过Tamarack，因为Qiu她小时候从来不会喜欢最好的闺蜜。他们有过美好的时光，也有过糟糕的时光。尤其是当Baxter开始与秋渐行渐远时，Qiu也开始与Tamarack渐行渐远。但如果MC从他们相遇的那一刻起就和Qiu很亲近，而且这种关系永远保持不变，那么相比之下，Baxter可能会觉得有点不好。

谢谢你的辛勤工作。你不知道我们有多感激。谢谢你。我的问题是:对于Our Life系列的主角——Cove、Tam和Qiu，你能透露他们什么时候喜欢上某人吗?比如，他们是自己保密还是即使他们试图隐瞒，大家也都知道?他们会因此感到尴尬吗?抱歉，如果这个问题已经回答了。

对Cove来说，这非常明显。每个人都知道这是无法隐藏的，即使他希望自己可以。对于Tamarack和Qiu来说，这取决于他们的年龄，但我不能确定，因为现在游戏的很多部分都不完整。

嗨！我对Our Life 2感到非常兴奋，并且想知道未来是否有Derek DLC的计划?

答案是否定的。这不在计划之内。这是一件很棒的事情，但我不知道它是否有可能实现，因为基础游戏已经变得更大更复杂了。所以现在，不，它不在计划中，不在预算中。但我同意如果根据我们的时间，这将是一个伟大的补充。

我不确定你现在是否能回答这个问题，但是你决定了Our Life 2的语音名称处理方式了吗?更具体地说，是否会对在Kickstarter上获得游戏配音的人数设置限制?如果你现在不能回答这个问题，我能理解。

这已经决定好了。Kickstarter对参与人数没有限制，但每个账户只能获得一个名字。我想，如果你在Kickstarter上注册了多个账户，你就可以避开这个问题，但你不可能先注册一个账户，然后再添加更多账户。我决定每个账户只收一个，这样我们就可以试着用这种方式限制一下。我不想，你知道，通过设定上限来正式限制它。所以，如果人们想要得到不止一个，那就很不方便了。

Our Life 2中会有生日活动吗?比如，我觉得如果我们都去买生日礼物，或者是MC的生日，那就太好了。别有压力。很明显，你做得太好了，我喜欢这些游戏。

谢谢你。不会有生日活动，因为生日每年都会发生，如果它发生在一个步骤中，它应该发生在所有四个步骤中。我不想为每一步都安排生日活动，所以很不幸，没有。

谢谢你的辛勤工作。你都做得太好了。谢谢你。关于Our Life 1男孩的问题：戴帽子还是不戴帽子?我想Derek不会介意戴棒球帽，而Baxter是个绅士，所以他可能会考虑戴高档的帽子。但是Cove，我无法完全想象除了在迫不得已下戴帽子，比如天气冷到需要戴无檐便帽的时候。

这是事实。baxter不会介意花哨的帽子，Derek不会介意运动型的帽子，在迫不得已的情况下，cove也不会介意戴帽子。

Cliff Sharp有名字吗，或者非官方的名字?

不，我完全没有想。

Our Life 1的男孩们是好司机吗?

是的，他们是好司机。如果让我选一个最好的，那可能是Baxter。

再次感谢你的辛勤工作。

好，也谢谢你。

嗨。希望你今天过得愉快。除了巴克斯特，还有其他角色有中间名吗?还有一个问题是:OL2的主角会像OL1那样有中间名吗?

答案是现在他们没有中间名，但是没错，其他角色有中间名。游戏中的主要角色，他们有中间名，Qiu和Tamara还没有，但他们会有的。但Cove有个中间名，叫James。Derek有个中间名，Rio。然后我们上一款游戏中的角色，XOXO Droplets，都有中间名。比如Jeremy，他在剧里只是个配角。他的中间名是Marion，Shiloh的中间名是Leon。他也是XOXO Droplets的。Nate也是，那个对Shiloh、Liz和MC大吼大叫的家伙，他的中间名是Archibald。

Think I’ll ask some questions this round. As always, thank you + team for your amazing work and dedication to your respective crafts, and for having this Q&A. Thank you for the kind words. My questions: I know you only have so much of Our Life 2 figured out at this point, but I wondered what you considered romance wise towards each step in general strokes? Naturally when the MC + Tamarack and Cho are younger, you want it to be age appropriate, but I’m wondering if each Step will be romantically significant with consideration to its age, or if we’ll have to work up to it as a whole? Will Step One be mild in that area, or will you have pinnacles like hand-holding or a kiss on the cheek, for example, perhaps even dating? Because kids at a young age play at romance, even if it’s not seriously emulating what they see. Respectively, what are you envisioning as general romantic plateaus between Steps? Do you think you’ll add more or do something different versus Our Life 1, especially with two leads to play around with? Thank you for answering what you can. I know it’s a lot! More so interested in your thought process than anything super concrete.

Well, I can say technically in a very general sense that since they are slightly older in Step One and Two than they were in Our Life 1, that there probably will be slightly more, I guess, crush based things that can happen because they’re not as little kids and not as little teenagers. But obviously I can’t give any specifics on it. And I’m honestly still trying to figure out exactly how I want it to work when there are two leads. Because, you know, how do you have basically a shared main story that doesn’t make their romances feel like they’re the same thing, just inserting a different person? So it’s less about having more options to play around with things, and more concern on how to make it still feel special and unique for each individual love interest if you do go down the love interest route with them. But I’m sure it’ll work out in the end eventually!

First time doing this, so I wanted to ask some questions that are more fun and non-serious since we don’t know a lot about OL2 yet. Would any of the OL2 cast use social media and would any of them be popular on social media during Step Two and forward? Since I figured 2014 was the peak of the Vine area, it’d be a fun question.

And I would say all of them use social media to a degree. Tamarack the least, and Serenity probably the most, but I can’t say specifically what they might be doing, which apps they prefer. That’s not really decided yet. Though none of them would be like especially notably popular on any social media. Like maybe one of them would have like a few hundred followers and that would be like a big deal in comparison to having like a dozen. But nobody’s like even mildly famous.

Qiu和Tamarack会做饭吗，还是说他们在厨房里不值得信任?我知道Tamarack很喜欢烘焙，但烘焙和烹饪是不一样的。

Tamarack更像是一个面包师，而不是厨师，但她能把某些菜做得很好，总的来说至少做得不错。Qiu是一个糟糕的面包师和一个好的厨师。

打架谁会赢?Renee还是熊?

这取决于Renee是有武器还是赤手空拳。如果是徒手格斗，熊就会赢，Renee就会逃跑。但如果Renee有什么东西可以保护自己，也许她能把熊吓跑。

嗨！我想知道Qiu和Tamarack是否可以单独成为亲密的朋友，而不考虑MC与他们中的任何一个的关系?有没有可能他们只是邻居？如果这个问题你还不能回答，我很抱歉。提前感谢。

MC和他们的关系不会影响他们自己的关系除非他们都在嫉妒。在这种情况下，MC可能会有额外的摩擦，但这不会对他们的关系造成太大的破坏。它只会增加一些额外的颠簸。但总的来说，他们的关系有自己的轨道，你无法真正改变它的发展方式。但不剧透的结局是，是的，他们会成为朋友，他们会成为亲密的朋友，所以这是永远不会错的。

非常重要——Our Life 1对兔子的看法是什么?如果一只非常可爱的兔子可能把他们的房子弄得一团糟，他们会有多烦恼?

虽然他们都认为兔子在理论上是非常可爱的，但没有人会喜欢兔子在他们的房子里捣乱。你知道，Cove可以用他自己的方式把自己的房子搞砸，他不会想让一只兔子来做这件事。Derek，他在某种程度上是个洁癖。他喜欢所有东西一弄脏就马上收拾干净。Baxter并不是一个爱打扫的人，尽管他确实想要一个干净的空间，他不会喜欢那些增加更多混乱的东西。所以他们三个人只有在积极地和MC生活在一起，并且MC帮忙照顾兔子的情况下才会拥有一只兔子。

青少年时期的Baxter会对他急躁的坏男孩形象被取笑/被质疑有多生气？这让我很难过，因为一群朋友会无情地取笑他说的大部分话。

这真的不会激怒他。他可以放弃，然后恢复过来，这不会影响他的自尊，因为他知道自己很奇怪，其他人会认为他在做奇怪的事情。但在他看来，这是一种福利。现在回想起来，他会觉得很尴尬，就像你在他成年后取笑他一样。然后他更为他的那个时代感到尴尬。

Next, we have two questions that are both about the best friend feature in Our Life 2. The first is: Hi! in Our Life 2 I know you can only be best friends with either Tamarack or Qiu. My question is, can that change, or is best friends a status for life kind of a thing? Like, for example, can you tell Tam you’re best friends in Step One, but then decide that you’re actually best friends with Qiu and Step Two? Thanks.

And the other one is: Hi! Thanks for all the hard work put into these games. They bring me great joy and comfort. I’m glad to see that. Sorry if this has been asked before. If we didn’t think that Qiu or Tamarack was our best friend in Step One, do we still have the option to be best friends in the future Steps? Or if we choose one of them as the best friend, can we change our opinion in a future Step?

And the answer is yes to all of that. You can start as best friends with one and then change to another in a future Step. Or you can start with no best friend and then choose the best friend in a future Step. It’s not the same as choosing love or family, where once you choose it, you can’t go back to not being love or considering them family. You can change who’s currently the best friend or if you even have a best friend. Though it has to be done in between Steps. There’s no breaking up of best friends in the middle of a Step.

Bit of a weird one this time. When writing a new character, do you always start at their earliest appearance and then come up with how they change and evolve as you go, or do you come up with a canon Step/age and then fill in the other steps later? For example, with Qiu, was their Step One version the first one created, or did you start with, say, their Step Two iteration and then wrote both Steps One and Three later?

And I do it entirely chronologically for these Our Life games. I definitely figure out who the characters are as they literally grow along with the story and get older later in development. So when developments early on, they are young and the later it goes on, the more I work on their older versions.

In Step One of Our Life 2, Opal already brought up how the MC can take extracurriculars after school in things like dance or learning an instrument. I was wondering if we were limited to just one class, or if we can take more than one, like one extracurricular is on one day of the week and then there’s another on a different day. So the MC can take both.

And at this point I can’t give any 100% guarantees because it hasn’t officially been written and finalized and finished in the game. But I would imagine that yes, you can have more than one extracurricular. But that will probably be because extracurriculars likely won’t come up too much. It’ll be like choosing a hobby where it’s only occasionally mentioned offhand, unless it’s something that you’re doing actively with the lead, like going to band with Tamarack, where in that case it can be more of an official scene. But if it’s just something random, there’s likely not going to be like backgrounds and scenes dedicated to whatever that extracurricular is.

To piggyback off the question about extracurriculars, is there a possibility that influencer tier patrons will be able to suggest extracurriculars they’d like to see, or vote on a poll for which ones are available?

And I doubt there will be polls, but people can suggest extracurriculars. Again, it’s likely not going to lead to significant additions to the events. It’ll probably just be like, “Oh yeah, you can choose to be a gymnast from the list.” And then occasionally the MC will be like, “Oh yeah, I’ve got to go to my gymnast class after school today,” and everyone will be like, “Okay, bye.” And then you go, but nothing actually happens. So you could choose what gets filled in there, but it’s not really going to lead to much else besides a mention.

How many DLCs can we expect? About the same or a smaller number?

And I imagine you’re talking about Our Life 2. Currently there are only three DLCs that are in the plan: one for Step One, one for Step Two, one for Step Three. And they will, like Our Life 1, add five additional Moments to those time periods. A stretch goal on the upcoming Kickstarter will be a wedding DLC, just like it was for Our Life 1. And then currently that is it. There are not any other DLC stretch goals planned since I would want to see how long everything takes and if we can actually keep to our schedule for the current DLC plan before promising to add any others. So even if we surpass the goal for the wedding DLC Kickstarter thing, I’m not going to put another DLC on the list just because it’s already going to be so much to get done, and I’d rather get more done before promising even more stuff.

As far as parenting in, Our Life 1, our moms are kind of team and share the “good cop/bad cop, fun mom/serious mom” depending on the scene. For Opal, does she embody both of those things, or is she mostly serious as a parent or mostly fun as a parent? Since she chose to be a single parent, how does she view her parenting and how will it kind of grow in the game?

I wouldn’t say Opal is exactly the fun kind of parent or the serious kind of paren. As a person, she’s sort of a very well restrained, self-composed kind of lady, and she can be charming and nice, but definitely not super silly. And the way she is with other people is mainly the way she is with her kids. She’s a little sweeter and more affectionate, but she’s still just kind of the same person she is overall with the MC, and she treats them basically like an equal and rarely uses sort of her parental authority as the reason for why things are. She usually tries to make a case for things should be this way because of this sensible reason. As for how that changes with time, I mean it’ll probably depend on how the MC is as a person. She’ll probably try to suit how she acts based on what they prefer. But, you know, it’ll also depend on what happens later in development. I haven’t gotten that far, so I don’t know for sure.

I don’t know if someone asked this already, but when are the OL gang’s plus Baxter and Derek’s birthdays?

The only people who have set birthdays are Cove, Derek, and Baxter. Cove’s is November 2nd, Derrick’s is September 29th, and Baxter is May 19th. Baxter and Derrick’s birthdays are the day their DLCs came out, and Cove’s was going to be the day the game came out, but then it got pushed back into the 16th and so his is slightly off. His birthday remained November 2nd even with that delay. Nobody else in Our Life 1 has a birthday, except for Jeremy and Shiloh because they are main characters from XOXO Droplets. Jeremy’s birthday is May 4th, and Shiloh’s birthday is October 30th.

Does that tropical place have a real name?

Also, No. Did not come up with names for those places.

Got a few questions. Not sure if some of them have been answered or can’t be answered, but here we go. Is Kyra’s hair naturally two-toned, or does she dye the ends of her hair?

It is naturally that color. It grows lighter, but then the older it is, the more the color fades and darkens and turns more blue.

Did Cliff already have the shop before the start of Step One, or did he establish his business after the move? I remember Kyra saying that she “remembers the ropes”, like she used to help him in the shop before.

Cliff founded his store when Cove was a toddler. So it was a good few years before Step One actually started.

For Step Four, mostly in Our Life 2, is there any consideration to do more “fade to black” implications for intimacy? Like the make out level we got in Our Life 1, but then anything further just cuts to the next day or some time later.

Wery likely not. Our Life’s base game, that’s publicly available, I don’t even really want to imply that there’s sex happening. It’s just not the tone for a game that’s safe for like younger teens as well. So I don’t even want to, like, mess around with people being like, “Oh, I don’t know. It might not be appropriate for younger people if it’s implying that their sex, even if it’s not shown.” So probably not.

Will Renee have a chance of being a love interest option in the DLC later on?

And it’s mentioned currently that’s not happening, but it’s not impossible.

Does Franky and Opal have a canonical location they lived before moving to Golden Grove, or will it be left in the air for players to decide where you previously lived?

It’s up to the player. There is no canon answer for where they came from.

Our Life 1和Our Life 2中所有对象的香料耐受程度是多少，从1分到10分?

我现在还不能回答Our Life 2，但Our Life 1都很不错。Baxter可能是最差的，只有6分。Cove的8分可能比他高，Derek是10分。

What first got you interested in writing stories?

Uh, very specifically, it was Harvest Moon, now known as Story of Seasons in English. But I didn’t enjoy writing most of my life until my late teens, when me and my friends on this Harvest Moon forum decided to make up our own Harvest Moon game. And I came up with a plot and all these fake character bios. And then I started writing like fake little heart events, like there are in the actual games. And then at that same time was when I was really getting into visual novels, playing them, like especially indie made visual novels. And I was like, “I bet I could make a visual novel with these Harvest Moon OS’s. And then you could actually, like play a little game and talk to them. Like, I couldn’t make a real farming game, but I could just make a visual novel that seems doable.” And then I did that, and that was the first VN I ever made. And then I just decided I really liked making visual novels. And it wasn’t about, you know, being for my OCs anymore. I just liked doing it and started coming up with my entirely own ideas in order to keep making visual novels because I had so much fun doing it. And so, yeah, I got into writing purely to make visual novels and purely because of Harvest Moon. So if anyone was wondering if that Blue Feather sticker was a reference to Harvest Moon and how you propose with a blue feather, yes, it is. That is an intentional reference.

I love reading through the questions in these threads. Some are just straight up funny. That said, what’s your favorite type of magic? Like it could really be anything, a spell or school of magic, or a concept like magic of theatre. And also what are things you find magical? Those are all my questions. I hope they’re fun to answer.

I guess if the magic of theatre counts, I do like theatre actually a lot. But I would say if those kinds of concept counts, then it would be like the magic of storytelling, because I really like stories. Most of what I do is either creating my own stories or reading/listening to other stories, you know, that’s like my favorite thing. So I would pick that if it counts. What are things that I find magical? I don’t know. I’m actually kind of a really dull person. I don’t find a lot of magic in the world around me, but it’s mostly just because I’m sort of neutral in general, but maybe like shooting stars. Those are pretty snazzy, I would say.

Hi! I just wanted to thank you and the GB team for creating OL1 and OL2. My question is, in Our Life 2 you have the option of crushing on either Qiu or Tam. I think that if you’re crushing on one of them, they also reciprocate the feelings. But is it possible for the other LI to have feelings for the player all on their own? Or if the player likes Qiu, for example, would Tam ever have a crush on the player? Like how in Our Life 1, Derek would like the player no matter what. Thank you for answering. I hope you have a lovely day and remember to drink water.

Thank you. You too. But the answer is no. Derek is a different situation because it’s a side story and because it’s always a crush no matter what. It’s not dependent on your choices or anything. We can’t have the two main leads always crushing on the MC, since a lot of people will want to just be friends with them. And we can’t make it so if you make certain choices, then they’ll like you without you liking them because then you have to be a certain person in order to get them to like you and experience that kind of story. So, it’s always a mutual situation where you like them, they like you. There is sort of a bond there. There’s an attachment and affection. And so, you know, it makes sense that there can be mutual feelings in that regard because they just have these positive experiences and feelings towards each other mutually. But there’s not a way to make it where you do things just so where you don’t have to like them, but they still like you. That won’t be possible.

In Our Life 2, are there any immediate dealbreakers for the leads? I could be misremembering that I recall reading that Qiu would not want to date someone who considers themselves a sexual orientation that doesn’t encompass potentially being attracted to someone who’s gender fluid, but would date Qiu was an exception. For example, a girl saying “I consider myself heterosexual, but I’ll make an exception for you.” Thank you again!

Um, that’s a little… I do know the conversation you’re talking about, and Qiu wouldn’t date someone who says that they’re straight and is trying to date them, but they would generally be an exception for someone where, like, “I only date women, but in this case, only for you, I will date you.” That’s something they would be fine with. But if you’re actually putting the label like, “Oh yeah, I’m a straight person, but I’m also dating this gender fluid person,” then Qiu would be like, “No, you’re treating me like I’m male, basically, and I’m very uncomfortable with that.” And so that would be a deal breaker. But those are really like the only deal breakers because as nice as it is, to make them as fleshed out as possible, at the end of the day, these are characters that exist to make as many real life people as happy as possible. And so, I’m not going to make them say like, “Oh, if you’re not into music, Tamarack will never love you.” Like, that’s just not going to happen. So it’s only really extreme like bigotry situations where it would be an actual deal breaker. Otherwise, you can be whoever you are or whoever you want to imagine your character is, and it will be totally fine because I care more about the people playing than sort of having super realistic love interests in that regard.

Hi! I was just wondering, since in the Our Life universe there is no homophobia or similar, is there still some kind of pride month or something? And also, in case there is, will we be able to express our sexuality via flags in our room or with bracelets, etc.? Thank you!

And homophobia does exist in Our Life universe. You’re just never confronted with it directly. But you know, Baxter’s parents, they are homophobic. And that’s one of the many reasons that he cuts them out of his life. It was my first attempt at having a more customizable MC that’s more about what the player wants to do rather than having a very set character. And I just wasn’t happy with how the features were used and how everything turned out. It actually was quite far along in development. I just never showed a lot of examples or screenshots from it because I was never 100% on board with how things were. And ultimately, I decided the effort to try and fix it just wasn’t worth it, especially because at that time I had a much better game going on in terms of systems with Our Life 1. And so Eyes of the Echo was mainly just gaining experience and learning lessons and unfortunately can’t ever be a fully released game.

And that’s the whole Q&A! Another quite long one. My voice is getting really tired, but thank you to everybody who sent in questions. I hope that was a fun time to read or listen to, and I’ll be back again next month for September’s Q&A. Take care, everybody!
